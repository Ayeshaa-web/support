Student Support Hub

Your Quick Access to Student Questions

Academic Support

How do I register for courses?
You can register for courses through the student portal during the registration period.
What should I do if a course I want to register for is full?
You can join the waiting list or consult your academic advisor for alternative options.
Where can I find my class schedule?
Your class schedule is available on the student portal under the "My Courses" section.
How do I calculate my GPA?
To calculate your GPA, divide the total grade points earned by the total credit hours attempted.

Examinations and Assessments

How do I register for exams?
You can register for exams through the examination section of the student portal.
What happens if I miss an exam?
If you miss an exam, you should contact your instructor immediately to discuss your options.
Where can I check my exam results?
Exam results can be found in the student portal under the "Grades" section.
How can I appeal a grade?
You can appeal a grade by submitting a formal appeal request through the academic office.

Technical Support

How can I reset my portal password?
To reset your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page of the student portal.
I’m unable to log into the student portal. What should I do?
If you can't log in, check your internet connection and ensure you're using the correct credentials.
Where can I download software required for my course?
Required software can be downloaded from the IT services section of the student portal.
How do I access the school's Wi-Fi network?
You can access the school's Wi-Fi network using your student credentials. Refer to the IT services for more details.

Library and Research

How do I access the digital library?
Access the digital library via the library section of the student portal using your login credentials.
How long can I borrow a book from the library?
You can borrow a book for up to three weeks, with the option to renew if there are no holds.
Where can I find citation guides for my research?
Citation guides are available in the library resources section on the student portal.
How do I reserve a book that’s currently unavailable?
You can reserve a book through the library's online catalog or by contacting the library staff.

Student Life and Campus Services

How do I apply for student accommodation?
Apply for accommodation through the student services section on the student portal.
Where can I find information about campus events?
Information about campus events can be found on the university's events page on the website.
How do I request a transcript?
You can request a transcript through the registrar's office or online via the student portal.
How can I access counseling services?
Counseling services can be accessed by visiting the student wellness center or through the student portal.
